About Save The Sheep

Save The Sheep is a clever and heartwarming strategy-puzzle game that transforms players into heroic shepherds. Your mission? Build defenses to protect your adorable flock of sheep from the relentless attacks of big, bad, hungry wolves. With limited resources and increasing challenges, every move counts as you work to outsmart your adversaries.

Strategic Gameplay

In Save The Sheep, your tools are thick wooden poles that you use to create fences. However, careless placement won’t work—resource management and tactical thinking are essential as you race against time to protect your sheep. Every decision matters:

  • Circle the Sheep: Enclose your flock safely to keep the wolves at bay.

  • Capture Wolves: Take the fight to the predators by trapping them inside your fences.

How to Play

  1. Join the Farm: Start defending your flock by putting poles into the ground.

  2. Draw the Fence: Use your mouse or finger to draw a fence around your sheep.

  3. Hammer Steady: Ensure each pole is securely placed to withstand wolf attacks.

  4. React Quickly: Decide your next move fast as impatient wolves invade your farm.

  5. Limited Resources: Manage the available poles per level to find creative solutions.

Challenging Levels

As you progress, more sheep need protection while more hungry wolves attack. The game’s escalating challenges push your puzzle-solving skills and strategic brain to the limit. Can you protect all the sheep and outsmart the wolves in time?

Why Play Save The Sheep?

  • Fun Strategy and Puzzle Elements: Develop creative tactics to protect your sheep with limited resources.

  • Increasing Difficulty: Levels grow more complex as wolves invade faster, keeping gameplay exciting.

  • Adorable Graphics: Cute sheep and mischievous wolves create an engaging visual experience.

  • Relax and Enjoy: Despite the challenges, the game provides relaxing yet addictive fun.

Are you ready to step into the shoes of a great shepherd and save your flock? The safety of your woolly friends depends on you!
